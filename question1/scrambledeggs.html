Scrambledeggs

This is one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously scramled eggs.

Ingredeients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 tbs creme

    • Method

    1. melt butter in frying pan over medium heat
    2. Gently mix the eggs and creme in a bowl
    3. Once the butter has melted add creme and eggs
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
    5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
